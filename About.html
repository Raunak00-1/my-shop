Who We Are

Rajesh Kirana Store is a trusted and well-known grocery store located in Teliyagarh, Goshainganj, Ayodhya. For more than 20 years, we have been proudly serving our customers with honesty, dedication, and high-quality products.

Our shop was founded and is managed by Mr. Bhola Nath, who believes that customer satisfaction is the real success of any business. Under his guidance, Rajesh Kirana Store has become a reliable destination for daily household needs.

What We Offer

We provide a wide range of essential products including rice, wheat, pulses, edible oils, spices, packaged foods, snacks, and other daily-use grocery items. Every product is carefully selected to ensure freshness, quality, and fair pricing.
